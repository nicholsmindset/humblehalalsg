## Overall Impression

A genuinely strong, distinctive product surface whose **component craft outruns its page-level composition.** The single biggest opportunity: the home page buries its one un-copyable differentiator (graduated "how certain is this halal claim?") under ~14 sections and competing owner/travel CTAs, and defers the trust "why" to a hover tooltip touch users never see.

## What's Working

1. **The graduated trust system is executed with integrity, end to end** — the defensible moat is visible in the UI, not just claimed in copy.
2. **Deep, un-fakeable domain fidelity** — live prayer strip, Ramadan bar, salaam onboarding, EN/BM, SG lexicon.
3. **Honest degradation & empty states** — not-a-photo fallback, no bare headings over empty grids, "N of M places lack a map location" note.

## Priority Issues

**[P1] Home dilutes its Persuade job with length + competing CTAs.** ~14 stacked sections; the food-seeker's search competes with "List your business," two travel CTAs, a dark business-CTA band, sponsored slots, newsletter. Works against PRODUCT.md principle #3 (decide fast, on a phone). Fix: relocate the business-CTA band to `/for-business`, collapse redundant trust treatments to one, tighten the section order. → **distill**

**[P2] The trust differentiator is under-sold above the fold.** The graduated-confidence USP is only a thin hero line + a slim strip far down. Fix: promote a compact badge-legend/confidence explainer directly under the hero. → **clarify**

**[P2] Explore toolbar overloaded for mobile.** Filters chip + Sort select + CertifiedToggle + List/Map toggle share one row at ~360px. Fix: move CertifiedToggle into the FilterPanel; keep Filters + view toggle + sort. → **layout**

**[P2] Trust "why" is a hover tooltip invisible on touch.** The reason behind a confidence tier lives in `hs-pill`'s `title` attribute — the peak trust moment on mobile shows only a colored dot + one word. Fix: surface the reasoning inline on the card (and/or a persistent legend). → **clarify**

**[P3] "Three hubs" copy contradicts four rendered cards** in `HomeFeatureSection`. → **clarify**

**[P3] Hero/eyebrow/pattern drift from tokens.** `.hero-h1` 700/−.02em/1.06 vs Spectral 600/−.01em/1.12; `.eyebrow` .72rem/.18em vs the .58rem/.22em label token; girih stroke hardcoded `#0F5C4A` vs the `#12525B` teal token. → **typeset**

## Persona Red Flags

**Jordan (first-timer):** "Certified / listed only" jargon before the badge system is taught; USP not explained above the fold; immediate overload (5 hero chips + 8 categories + 9 area tiles); "three hubs / four cards" reads as sloppiness.

**Casey (distracted mobile):** overloaded Explore toolbar; the raised center bottom-nav slot serves "Add listing" (owner action) while search — the core action — is a side tab; long scroll past two dark bands + sponsored slot.

**SG Muslim food-seeker at the trust decision:** the confidence-tier reason is a touch-invisible tooltip; self-declared vs certified leans on a dot color with no inline legend; the strongest reassurance (deep-link to the HalalSG record) is only on the detail page, not at the scanning stage.

## Minor Observations

- Prelaunch banner + business-CTA h2 use hardcoded `#fff` + inline weights (off-token; pure white nudges the Tinted-Neutral rule).
- A full `TrustStrip` component exists but Home uses `home-trust-slim` — likely dead/duplicate; consolidate.
- `.chip.active svg { color: var(--gold) }` faithfully implements the "leading icon turns saffron" spec; `save-fab` is 44×44 (good tap target).

## Questions to Consider

1. If graduated confidence is the moat, why does the hero lead with a generic "Find halal food…" instead of *demonstrating* "how certain?" above the fold?
2. Whose home page is this — the food-seeker's or the owner's? A dark business-CTA band **and** the center bottom-nav slot both serve owners.
3. At the mobile trust-decision moment, is a legend-less colored dot enough, or should the "why" be inline on the card?
